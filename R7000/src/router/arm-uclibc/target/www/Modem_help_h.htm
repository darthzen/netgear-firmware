Setup Wizard Help

After connecting the NETGEAR mobile broadband router into your network, you must configure it. You can do this using the Setup Wizard -- where the wizard will attempt to make the connection to the Internet service through your ISP -- or you can configure the router manually. It is recommended that, if you aren't used to setting up Internet connections, you let the Setup Wizard do some of the work for you.

To configure the NETGEAR mobile broadband router, you'll use the information that you filled out earlier in the Installation Guide.

To get started, click Next.
